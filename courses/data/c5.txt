DESC|A comprehensive course in Reformed (Calvinist) theology, presented sympathetically and in depth — covering Scripture, the doctrine of God, the covenants, the person and work of Christ, the doctrines of grace, the church, and the last things, then the history of the tradition from Augustine to Bavinck. Confessionally Reformed in conviction, but intellectually honest: it engages the strongest objections and represents other Christian traditions fairly where they differ.
