TITLE|Heretics and Visionaries

DESC|A study of the non-conformists who stood against the settled wisdom of their time, in science, religion, art, and politics. What does it take to see what everyone else has missed? When is the heretic a prophet, and when a crank? An honest look at the pattern of vision, dissent, persecution, and vindication — including, unflinchingly, the heretics who were dangerously wrong.
